NOTES:

- All models have negative R² scores (-0.087 to -0.014)
- This indicates that treating the problem as regression (continuous coordinates) is problematic
- Approach of using classification + mapping to coordinates is the right strategy
- The regression metrics (MAE, MSE, R²) are less relevant than classification metrics (F1, precision, recall)

2.  Recall is Critical for Political Ideology Mapping
= High recall ensures comprehensive coverage of ideological signals
- Missing tweets (false negatives) creates gaps in political compass visualization

3. The Precision-Recall Trade-off
- Models with very high precision (IndoRoBERTa: 0.8034) sacrifice recall
- For exploratory political analysis, it's better to capture more signals (high recall) than to be overly conservative (high precision)
